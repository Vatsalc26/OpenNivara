# Study Coach Pack

Welcome to the **Study Coach Pack** for OpenNivara! This pack includes configurations tailored for students and autodidacts looking to absorb complex technical material quickly.

## What's Included

- **Preferences**: Warm, supportive learning preferences, using metaphors and avoiding pure structural academic formulas.
- **Contexts**: Feynman learning technique guidance to ground abstract concepts in the real-world.
- **Style Guidelines**: Supportive, beginner-friendly communications presets.
- **Theme**: Warm Dark Green Calm Focus interface.
- **Command Snippets**:
  - `Feynman Technique Breakdown`
  - `Suggest Study Milestones`
