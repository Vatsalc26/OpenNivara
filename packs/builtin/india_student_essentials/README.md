# India Student Essentials

India Student Essentials is a data-only OpenNivara Skills v1 pack for practical learning support. It helps students in school, college, self-study and exam preparation build realistic plans, revise with active recall, understand mistakes, clarify concepts and stay consistent without unsafe pressure.

Installing this pack does not activate any behavior. After installing it from Store, enable individual skills in Settings -> Skills.

## Included Skills

- India Study Plan Builder: turns academic goals, subjects, available time, backlog and constraints into weekly or multi-week plans.
- India Daily Timetable Builder: creates sustainable daily routines around school, college, coaching, commute, breaks, responsibilities and sleep.
- India Revision Cycle Planner: builds spaced revision and active-recall workflows after material has been learned.
- India Mock Test Analyzer: converts actual test evidence into mistake buckets and next-mock experiments.
- India Weak Topic Diagnosis: separates weak topics into concept, recall, application, speed, reading, careless-error and confidence patterns.
- Notes to Recall Converter: transforms student-provided notes into summaries, recall questions, flashcards and revision sheets.
- India Doubt Explainer: explains a supplied concept or question step by step without assuming board, exam or syllabus context.
- India Exam Stress Consistency Coach: offers supportive consistency planning for exam stress, guilt, burnout and disrupted routines.

## Boundaries

- The pack is data-only and grants no executable code.
- Skills do not enable shell access, file writes, file reads, app opening or network requests.
- Skills do not automatically read local academic files. Notes conversion works only from content the user provides in the message or chat context.
- Skills do not retrieve live official information, dates, syllabi, eligibility rules, counselling rules, deadlines, cutoffs, seats or government/education-platform details.
- Skills do not promise marks, ranks, admission, selection, score jumps or guaranteed outcomes.
- Themes remain UI-only and do not change assistant behavior.
- Stress-support guidance is supportive study consistency help. It is not medical care, mental-health diagnosis, counselling or crisis response.
